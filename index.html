About Us

Mount Vista features mountain views, free WiFi and free private parking, located in Pretoria, 13 km from Union Buildings. The property has garden views and is 16 km from University of Pretoria and 17 km from Voortrekker Monument. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk and a business centre for guests.

At the guest house, every unit includes a desk and a flat-screen TV. All the units are equipped with bed linen and towels.

Guests at the guest house can enjoy hiking nearby, or make the most of the garden. Pretoria Country Club is 20 km from Mount Vista, while Rietvlei Nature Reserve is 30 km away. The nearest airport is Wonderboom Airport, 8 km from the accommodation.

Does Mount Vista provide laundry Services ?

Yes! Mount Vista Guest House offers laundry service at your grasp for whenever you need your favourite clothes clean and fresh all throughout your holiday. Please contact the property for more information.

What time is check-in and check-out at Mount Vista Guest House ?

Check-in is from 02:00 PM, and check-out is until 10:00 AM.
You may request early check-in or late check-out during booking, subject to availability. Travelers checking in or out before or after the specified periods may be charged an additional fee.

Does Mount Vista Guest House offer free cancellation for a full refund ?

Yes, Mount Vista Guest House does have fully refundable room rates available to book on our site. If you've booked a fully refundable room rate, this can be cancelled up to a few days before check-in depending on the property's cancellation policy.
