About Us

Mount Vista features mountain views, free WiFi and free private parking, located in Pretoria, 13 km from Union Buildings. The property has garden views and is 16 km from University of Pretoria and 17 km from Voortrekker Monument. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk and a business centre for guests.

At the guest house, every unit includes a desk and a flat-screen TV. All the units are equipped with bed linen and towels.

Guests at the guest house can enjoy hiking nearby, or make the most of the garden. Pretoria Country Club is 20 km from Mount Vista, while Rietvlei Nature Reserve is 30 km away. The nearest airport is Wonderboom Airport, 8 km from the accommodation.
